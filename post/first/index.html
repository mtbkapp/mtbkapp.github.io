First Post

19 Aug 2014

I’ve wanted to have a blog for a while now and am finally getting to it. Do I care if anyone reads it? Nope. It’s mainly for me to remember things that I’ve done that I think are interesting. I guess it’s possible for someone to stumble across this and find something useful. It’s also possible that no one besides me will ever read it.

I met spf13 at Strange Loop last year and found out that he has a cool and fast static site generator called Hugo. I decided to use it for this blog. Also I’m currently not that great at CSS so I used Kube for that. I think Hugo and Kube and a lot of other things are pretty dang cool and am glad that their authors have shared their work.
